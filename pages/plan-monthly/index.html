Scope of the Monthly Sprint

Our Monthly Sprint Plan includes:

  • Regular testing cycles covering functionality, performance, user experience, and security.
  • Identification, prioritization, and tracking of bugs and issues.
  • Collaboration with your development team for swift issue resolution.
  • Automated testing to ensure stability and quality.
  • Strategic recommendations to enhance QA processes.

Methodology

We adopt an Agile-inspired approach to ensure efficiency and transparency:

  • Weekly sprints with regular meetings and reports.
  • Use of industry-standard tools for testing and bug tracking (e.g., Jira, Selenium).
  • Continuous communication via Slack or email for real-time updates.

Deliverables

Each month, you receive:

  • Weekly progress reports summarizing identified issues and actions taken.
  • A comprehensive monthly report with all detected issues, performance metrics, and UX feedback.
  • A prioritized bug list and strategic recommendations for improvement.

Duration

The Monthly Sprint Plan spans 30 consecutive working days, with weekly updates and a final monthly report.
