Audit Plan - Checklayer

Welcome to Checklayer, a Netherlands-based micro-agency specializing in Quality Assurance (QA) testing. Our Audit Plan is a one-time, in-depth service designed to evaluate your software product’s quality, provide detailed insights into its current state, and offer actionable recommendations—all within 1-3 days. We ensure your project meets the highest standards, delivering professional results tailored to your needs.

Scope of the Audit

Our Audit Plan covers the following key areas to give you a comprehensive overview of your product:

  • Code Quality: A review of your codebase to ensure it’s clean, maintainable, and scalable.
  • Performance: Testing the speed, responsiveness, and stability of your application.
  • User Experience: Assessing usability and accessibility to enhance user satisfaction.
  • Security: Identifying vulnerabilities that could put your product at risk.
  • Basic Critical Checks: Conducting essential tests to catch critical issues early.

Methodology

We combine proven QA techniques to deliver a thorough and efficient audit:

  • Manual Testing: Hands-on evaluation to uncover issues automated tools might overlook.
  • Automated Testing: Using industry-standard tools for quick and accurate checks (e.g., regression and load testing).
  • Code Review: Detailed analysis of your code for best practices and error detection.
  • Performance Analysis: Benchmarking your application against performance standards.
  • UX Testing: Simulating user interactions to ensure an intuitive experience.

Deliverables

At the end of the Audit, you’ll receive:

  • A detailed report highlighting issues, strengths, and areas for improvement.
  • Actionable recommendations to address identified problems.
  • A concise plan to implement fixes and optimize your product.
