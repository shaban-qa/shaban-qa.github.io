Our Services

At Checklayer, we provide tailored QA solutions for startups, small businesses, and indie developers. We catch bugs before your users do, ensuring your web and mobile products are polished and reliable.

What we offer:

- Quick audits to spot critical issues in days.

Our Quick Audit service is perfect for teams needing a fast yet thorough review of their product’s core functionality. In just 1–2 days, we identify critical bugs that could impact your users’ experience. You’ll receive a detailed bug report and a video walkthrough explaining the issues and suggested fixes.

- Sprint-based testing for agile teams.

Our Sprint QA plan is designed for agile teams working in sprints. Over one week, we perform functional and regression testing, API checks, and even set up basic automation scripts to ensure your sprint goals are met without quality compromises.

- Full-cycle QA for a month of thorough coverage.

Our Full Month plan offers comprehensive QA support for 30 days. We cover end-to-end testing, including performance, security, and usability checks. You’ll also get automation scripts and CI/CD integration to streamline your development process.

We combine manual precision with automation tools to deliver actionable insights—fast. Let us help you ship with confidence.
